## 🌍 About the Event

**AAMRO** (*14th Orano-Eurelian Meeting of Rheumatology & 2nd International Congress*) is a prestigious international gathering dedicated to advancing the field of rheumatology.

Held in the heart of **Oran**, this event brings together leading experts, researchers, and healthcare professionals to share knowledge, innovations, and scientific excellence.
