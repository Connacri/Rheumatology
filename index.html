About The Congress

Advancing Rheumatology in the Mediterranean Region

The 14th Orano-Eurelian Meeting of Rheumatology and 2nd International Congress brings together leading experts, researchers, and clinicians to discuss the latest advances in rheumatology and autoimmune diseases.

Department Rheumatology

Hospital Establishment Dr Medjbeur Tami Aïn El Turck Oran

🏆

14th

Edition

A Premier Gathering for Rheumatology Professionals

Organized under the auspices of the People's Democratic Republic of Algeria, Ministry of Health, Directorate of Health and Population of Oran, this congress represents a landmark event in the field of rheumatology.

The Orano-Eurelian Meeting of Rheumatology has established itself as a key platform for scientific exchange, bringing together practitioners from Algeria, Europe, and beyond to share knowledge and innovations in diagnosis and treatment.

Scientific Topics

Congress Scientific Themes

Explore the cutting-edge topics that will be discussed during this 3-day international congress.

🦴

Rheumatoid Arthritis

Latest advances in the management and treatment of Rheumatoid Arthritis, including biologic therapies and targeted synthetic DMARDs.

🔬

Spondyloarthritis

Axial and peripheral spondyloarthritis: from pathogenesis to personalized treatment strategies.

🧬

Autoimmune Diseases

Systemic Lupus Erythematosus, Sjögren's syndrome, and other autoimmune connective tissue diseases.

💊

Osteoporosis

Osteoporosis diagnosis, prevention, and treatment. Bone mineral density assessment and fracture risk evaluation.

🩺

Osteoarthritis

Osteoarthritis management, regenerative medicine approaches, and emerging therapeutic targets.

🧪

Vasculitis & Rare Diseases

Systemic vasculitides, rare rheumatologic conditions, and orphan drug development.

📊

Immunotherapy & Biologics

Biosimilars, novel biologic agents, and the future of immunotherapy in rheumatologic practice.

💎

Crystal Arthropathies

Gout and pseudogout: diagnosis, management, and prevention of crystal-induced arthritis.

Pediatric Rheumatology

Juvenile idiopathic arthritis and other pediatric rheumatologic conditions: early diagnosis and treatment.

Oran Bay Accor Hotel

Oran, Algeria

A Premier Conference Destination

The Oran Bay Accor Hotel is a state-of-the-art conference facility located on the Mediterranean coast of Oran, Algeria. With panoramic views of the bay and modern meeting spaces, it provides the perfect setting for this prestigious rheumatology congress.

The hotel offers easy access from Oran International Airport and is surrounded by the city's rich cultural heritage and beautiful coastline.
