Welcome Message

Message from the Congress President

Dear Colleagues, Distinguished Guests, and Friends, It is my great honor and sincere pleasure to welcome you to the 14th Orano-Eurelian Meeting and the 2nd International Congress of Rheumatology taking place from April 23rd to 25th, 2026. This scientific gathering represents a unique opportunity to strengthen collaboration between national and international experts, and to foster the exchange of knowledge in the ever-evolving field of Rheumatology.

Over the years, this meeting has grown into a dynamic platform where clinicians, researchers, and healthcare professionals come together to share experiences, discuss challenges, and explore the latest advances in diagnosis and treatment. The 2026 edition is particularly meaningful, as it reflects our continued commitment to excellence in patient care, research innovation, and medical education. Our program has been carefully designed to address key topics in Rheumatology, including inflammatory diseases, degenerative disorders, osteoporosis, and emerging therapeutic strategies.

We are proud to host distinguished speakers and renowned experts who will contribute to enriching discussions and high-level scientific sessions. This congress also provides a valuable space for young physicians and trainees to engage, learn, and present their work, ensuring the future of our specialty. Beyond the scientific content, we hope this meeting will strengthen professional ties and encourage lasting collaborations across borders. Oran, with its rich cultural heritage and warm hospitality, offers an ideal setting for this important event.

I would like to extend my special thanks to Dr. Medjadi Mohsine for his outstanding dedication and invaluable efforts in making this congress a success, and to all members of the organizing and scientific committees for their dedication and hard work. My sincere thanks also go to our partners and sponsors for their invaluable support. To all participants, your presence and contribution are the true driving force behind the success of this congress.

I encourage you to actively participate in the sessions, share your insights, and make the most of this exceptional opportunity. Together, let us continue advancing Rheumatology for the benefit of our patients and our communities. I wish you a fruitful congress and a pleasant stay in Oran.

Pr. Zahia Lamri

Congress President

AAMRO President Message

Message from the AAMRO President

Ladies and Gentlemen, dear colleagues and friends, It is a great pleasure and honor to welcome you to Oran for the 14th Orano-Eurelian Meeting and our 2nd International Congress of Rheumatology.

I would like to warmly thank our distinguished speakers who have joined us from various countries. Your presence greatly enriches the scientific value of this congress.

My sincere thanks also go to all participants for your strong attendance and interest, as well as to our pharmaceutical partners for their valuable support in making this event possible.

The aim of this congress is to foster exchange, share experience, and update our knowledge in rheumatology, a field that continues to evolve rapidly. We hope these days will provide an excellent opportunity for learning, discussion, and collaboration.

I wish you all a very productive and successful congress.

Dr. Medjadi Mohsine

President of AAMRO

In Memoriam

Tribute to Our Pioneers

Tribute

Nadera Graine

To speak of and to remember a friend and colleague is always a difficult task for the heart. It is said that when a life fades away, an entire universe disappears — a universe of feelings, emotions, love, and memories.

Nadera Graine was deeply cherished by all. With her ever-present smile, she gradually became widely recognized within the community of rheumatologists across the country, through numerous scientific meetings and professional travels.

After a long illness, which she faced with remarkable courage and resilience, never ceasing her work, she left us, leaving behind her daughter, her son, and two grandchildren.

Nadera, if you can hear us where you now rest, all of us your family and colleagues wish you eternal peace.

In Memoriam

Our Beloved Colleagues

Their passing has left a void that neither time nor circumstance can fill. Yet, we choose to honor their memory with gratitude and quiet reflection, celebrating the lasting impact they have had on our lives and our work.

We will cherish their memory with the utmost care, as one preserves something truly invaluable, carrying it forward through our actions, our dedication, and our shared purpose.

They will be deeply missed; however, their presence endures — in the lives they touched, in the knowledge they shared, and in the spirit that continues to guide us each day.
